# Privacy Policy (heriO Chat App)

Last updated: 2026-03-30

This Privacy Policy explains how we process your data when you use **heriO** (the “App”).

## 1) Controller and Contact
App developer: **necatisen**  
Contact email: **heriohelp@gmail.com**

## 2) Data We Collect / Process
Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following types of data:

- **Account information**: (e.g., email address, username) — for account creation and sign-in.
- **In-app content**: (e.g., messages / posts) — to provide the core functionality of the App.
- **Location data**: (approximate or precise) — for location-based features (if you grant permission).
- **Camera and photos/media**: — to take/select photos and similar features (if you grant permission).
- **Device and diagnostics data**: (e.g., device model, OS version, crash logs) — for debugging and performance improvements.
- **Notification identifiers**: (push notification token) — to send notifications (if you grant permission).

## 3) Why We Process Data (Purposes)
- To operate the App and manage accounts and sessions
- To provide core features such as messaging/communication
- To send notifications (if you grant permission)
- To detect issues, improve security, and prevent abuse
- To improve App performance

## 4) Sharing of Data
We may share your data in the following cases:

- **Service providers**: vendors that help us run the App.
  - **Supabase**: for backend services such as authentication, database, and storage.
- **Legal obligations**: with competent authorities when required by law.

If you do not use an ad network or analytics/crash services, this section should be updated accordingly.

## 5) Permissions
The App may request the following permissions (depending on features):
- **Location**: for location-based features
- **Camera**: for taking photos/videos
- **Photos/Media**: for selecting from your gallery
- **Notifications**: to send notifications

You can change permissions at any time in your device settings.

## 6) Data Retention
We retain data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. If you delete your account, your data will be deleted or anonymized unless we are required to keep it for legal obligations.

## 7) Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8) Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take appropriate steps.

## 9) Your Rights and Requests
For requests regarding your data (access, correction, deletion, etc.), please contact us at **heriohelp@gmail.com**.

## 10) Changes
